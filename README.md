# CPI-Inflation-CaseStudy
CPI Inflation Case Study analyzing trends across all categories (Food, Transport, Housing, etc.) to identify top contributors, seasonal patterns, and insights. Includes Excel workbook with raw data, calculations, and visualizations for decision-making.

## Business Problem
- Analyze CPI inflation trends across all categories: Food, Transport, Housing, Healthcare, Education, etc.
- Identify which categories contribute most to overall inflation.
- Provide actionable insights for policy or business decisions.

## Dataset
- Single Excel workbook with multiple sheets:
  - Raw Data (all categories)
  - Business Problem
  - Calculations / KPIs

## Steps Taken
1. Understanded the business problem
2. Explored the dataset for all categories
3. Calculated CPI inflation metrics for each category
4. Performed month-over-month and year-over-year analysis
5. Derived insights and recommendations

## Key Insights
- Food, Transport, and Housing contributed the most to inflation
- Seasonal trends were observed in Food and Transport
- Recommended focus areas for cost management and policy planning

## Tools Used
Excel,  SQL , Visualization in Excel
